Target Projects
I worked for Target from 2011 - 2015 as a part of a small interactive team that built tools and applications for use within the company. Below is a small selection of projects I was lucky enough to be a part of and able to show.
RED Digital Magazine
Helped design the interface and article templates for the transition from print to digital for RED Magazine, a periodical for Target employees.
ServiceNow Icons
Icons created for an internal ticketing tool to report technology issues within the company.
In A Snap iOS App
An app that lets you instantly shop Target items directly off pages of magazines, billboards and other printed material. My role was to design the user interface of this app.
